Neighborhood Wayfinding

A visitor-friendly navigation tool for finding buildings, units, and entrances in a residential community.

Welcome

This prototype helps first-time visitors quickly search for a location, view the community map, and follow simple directions to the correct building and unit.

Core tasks in this prototype

1. Search for a building, unit, or office

2. View the neighborhood map and identify the target location

3. Follow step-by-step directions to the destination

Digital Prototype Version 1 • Based on the paper prototype for the neighborhood signage/navigation project
